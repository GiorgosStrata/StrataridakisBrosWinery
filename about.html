Our Story

Founded by the Strataridakis brothers, our winery is a testament to family, tradition, and a deep love for winemaking. Located in Crete, Greece, we combine time-honored techniques with modern innovation to craft wines that capture the essence of our land.

Our vineyards are tended with care, ensuring every grape tells a story of our heritage. Join us in celebrating the art of wine, passed down through generations.
